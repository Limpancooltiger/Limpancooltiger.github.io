Project Overview

During the second year at Jönköping University they have a course called NFK, which means that we do an internship at a company that we find.‍ I did my Internship at Combitech with eight other engineering students and together we created a hexapod robot that is remotely controlled by an android application.

My Contributions

Togheter with two other mechanical engineering students I design and created the prototype. The prototype is made by laser-cut plastic sheets and standard components. To do this I used Solidworks and alot of work in the workshop.
