Electric Guitar

Gymnasiearbete

Project Overview

During my last year at Bäckadalsgymnasiet we had a course where we could create whatever we wanted. One of my hobbies is to play guitar so I had always wanted to create my own guitar so that is what I did.

My Contributions

The shape is inspired by the telecaster guitar and the goal was to make the body transparent. To do this I made a 3D model in Soldiworks and learned how to use a CNC machine to create it. I bought a guitar to get a neck and the electric components that I wired to fit the new body.

What I learned

This was the first project that I did with the skills I had learned during gymnasiet. It was a learning curve to make 3D models that actually can be manufactured. I did research on materials and how to cut them with a CNC machine, also how to use an CNC machine.‍‍

Skills used

  • SolidWorks
  • CNC-Machining
  • Material Selection
  • Electrical Work
