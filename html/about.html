My passion for engineering

I've had a fascination with engineering for as long as I can remember. It all began with those childhood days playing with Legos. There was something truly captivating about building things from scratch and trying to understand how they functioned.Then, when I was about ten years old, I started playing computer games on my parents' computer, and my curiosity for software and IT began. I couldn't help but explore the inner workings of that metal box. Somewhere I've also grown a passion for cars and motorcycles, the exact moment it ignited remains a bit of a mystery. Nonetheless, these interests have naturally led me to pursue a path in mechanical engineering, particularly focusing on product development and design.
