Aug 2016 — Jun 2018

The natural path for me was to pursue a technological education during my time in high school, or "gymnasiet" as we call it. I initially enrolled at Erik Dahlbergsgymnasiet with a focus on information and media, which provided me with a solid foundation in computer fundamentals, including programming, web development, and hardware. However, I didn't quite find my place at that school, so for my final year, I made the switch to Bäckadal. This institution had a more pronounced emphasis on mechanical engineering, and it was there that I quickly developed a passion for computer-aided design (CAD) and hands-on work. Consequently, I made the decision to pursue a degree in mechanical engineering at Jönköping University.
